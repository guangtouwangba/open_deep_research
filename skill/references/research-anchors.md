# Research Domain — Authority Sources

## Academic Sources
- arXiv preprints (cs, stat, econ, q-fin)
- Google Scholar top-cited papers
- Nature/Science review articles
- IEEE/ACM conference proceedings (ICML, NeurIPS, SIGMOD, etc.)

## Industry Research
- Gartner Magic Quadrant & Hype Cycle
- McKinsey Global Institute reports
- BCG Henderson Institute
- 行业白皮书 (from leading companies)

## Technology Trends
- ThoughtWorks Technology Radar (bi-annual)
- CNCF Landscape & Annual Survey
- Stack Overflow Developer Survey (annual)
- GitHub Octoverse Report (annual)

## Verification Rules
- Paper must have DOI or arXiv ID
- Statistics must link to original report/survey with methodology
- Industry report must identify publisher, date, and sample size
- Claims about trends must cite data from last 2 years
